## Financial Dashboard Insights

- Sales and expenses are tracked by Business Unit (Software, Hardware, Advertising) and Scenario (Actuals, Budget, Forecast).
- Total expenses are categorized by Account, making it easy to identify major cost drivers.
- Monthly sales trends highlight actual performance versus budget and forecast.
- Net profit analysis shows the most profitable business units.
- Pivot tables and charts in Excel provide a clear visual summary of financial performance.
- SQL queries validate totals and net profit calculations, ensuring accuracy.
